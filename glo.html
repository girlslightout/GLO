ABOUT GLO

-Girls Light Out-

Welcome to GLO, the new innovative way to find light in the darkness. With GLO bags you will be able to find anything in your bag at night. Instead of searching for a flashlight, you will be able to see what is inside as soon as your GLO bag is opened. Don’t waste your time searching.
Use GLO.
